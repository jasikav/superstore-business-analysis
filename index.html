Superstore Business Analysis

End-to-end retail analytics project

By Jasika Verma

Project Overview

This project analyzes a retail superstore dataset to understand sales performance, profitability, and customer segments. The goal is to identify business insights and recommend actions to improve profitability.

Key Insights

  • Technology category generates the highest profit.
  • Furniture shows low profit margins despite strong sales.
  • Corporate customers contribute significantly to profitability.
  • Some products consistently generate losses.

Business Recommendations

  • Increase focus on high-margin Technology products.
  • Review pricing and discounts in the Furniture category.
  • Strengthen retention strategies for Corporate customers.
  • Re-evaluate or discontinue loss-making products.
