Executive Summary

This project analyzes the business performance of a retail superstore using transaction-level sales data.

Key Findings

  • The business generated over $2.3M in total sales with approximately $286K in profit.
  • Technology is the most profitable category, contributing both high sales and strong margins.
  • Furniture shows relatively low profit margins despite high sales, indicating cost or discount issues.
  • The Consumer segment drives volume, while Corporate customers contribute strong profitability.
  • A small set of products is responsible for a significant portion of total losses.

Business Recommendations

  • Focus marketing and inventory investments on Technology products.
  • Review pricing and discount strategies for Furniture category.
  • Strengthen retention strategies for Corporate customers.
  • Re-evaluate or reprice loss-making products to improve overall profitability.
