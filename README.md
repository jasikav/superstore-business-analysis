# Sales Performance & Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem
A retail company wanted to analyse sales performance across regions and product categories to identify underperforming areas and improve profitability.

---

## Objective
- Analyse revenue trends
- Identify low-performing regions
- Evaluate product performance
- Generate business recommendations

---

## Dataset Information
- Dataset Size: ~10,000 records
- Tools Used: SQL, Excel, Power BI

---

## Key Analysis Performed
- Revenue trend analysis
- Regional sales comparison
- Product category performance
- KPI tracking dashboard

---

## Key Insights
- Certain regions contributed ~15% lower sales
- Product performance varied significantly across categories
- Monthly sales trends showed seasonal fluctuations

---

## Recommendations
- Improve focus on underperforming regions
- Align inventory planning with seasonal demand
- Optimize product strategies for low-performing categories
