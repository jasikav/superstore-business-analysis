## Overview
This project presents an end-to-end business analytics case study on a retail superstore using transaction-level sales data.  
The objective is to analyze sales performance, profitability, and customer behavior, and derive actionable business insights.

The project is implemented using **Python, Pandas, and Matplotlib**, and deployed as a **live portfolio website using GitHub Pages**.

## Dataset
The dataset contains order-level retail transaction data including:
- Order and customer details
- Product categories and sub-categories
- Sales, profit, quantity, and discounts
- Regional and segment information

---

## Key Insights
- The business generated **~$2.3M in total sales** with **~$286K in profit**
- **Technology** is the most profitable category
- **Furniture** shows high sales but weaker profit margins
- Consumer customers drive volume, while Corporate customers drive profitability
- A small number of products contribute disproportionately to losses

---

## Business Recommendations
- Increase focus on Technology products through targeted marketing
- Re-evaluate pricing and discount strategies for Furniture
- Strengthen retention strategies for Corporate customers
- Identify and optimize or remove loss-making products
